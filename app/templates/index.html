Şubat 2024'ten beri Sanayi/Sektör Odaklı Bütünleşik Program (MasterBee) kapsamında yüksek lisans dersleri alarak akademik yönden kendimi geliştirmeye devam ediyorum. {% else %} I was born on September 16, 2001, in Tokat. After completing my high school education at PEMA College in 2020, I began my academic journey in the Computer Engineering department at Istanbul Technical University. Currently, I am a third-year student continuing my education. Aligned with my interests, I engage in research to enhance my understanding of machine learning concepts, particularly in recommendation systems and feature engineering. In July 2023, I was elected as the chairman of the board of directors at ITU Supercomputer Club. In line with the club's goals of disseminating technical topics such as supercomputers, high-performance computing, and parallel programming to an undergraduate level and increasing knowledge and culture in these areas, I continue to work with club members. Additionally, since February 2024, I have been taking master’s level courses as part of the Industry/Sector-Focused Integrated Program (Mas- terBee) to further enhance my academic growth. {% endif %}
